Webb Energy Corp. Overview

Webb Energy Corp. is a leading energy company based in Texas, specializing in crude oil, natural gas, and lithium production. Founded on a strong commitment to sustainable energy, we focus on the exploration, production, and distribution of these valuable resources to fuel economic growth and meet the world's increasing demand for energy.

Crude Oil Production

Crude oil makes up the majority of Webb Energy's business, accounting for 75% of our production. We operate multiple oil fields in Texas and are continuously expanding our infrastructure to meet the growing demand for this critical energy resource. Our operations are powered by advanced technology, ensuring efficient and safe extraction processes.

Natural Gas and Lithium

Natural gas is an essential part of our energy portfolio, contributing to 20% of Webb Energy's overall production. We are committed to harnessing its clean-burning potential to meet energy demands while reducing environmental impact. Additionally, Webb Energy has ventured into the lithium market, accounting for 5% of our business, as the world increasingly turns to lithium for batteries and renewable energy solutions.

Company Overview

Our oil and gas company was founded in 2006 in Texas. Since then, we've expanded our operations across North America, focusing on environmentally responsible energy production. With a robust portfolio of assets, we provide reliable energy to thousands of customers.

Mission & Values

We strive to meet the world’s growing energy needs while ensuring environmental stewardship and corporate responsibility. We are committed to innovation, safety, and the well-being of the communities we serve.
